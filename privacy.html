Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our social media management services:

1.1 OAuth Tokens

When you connect your social media accounts, we store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by third-party platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). These tokens are used exclusively to post content on your behalf as directed by you.

1.2 Basic Account Information

We may access and store basic account information from connected platforms, which may include:

  • Profile name and username
  • Profile picture
  • Account ID / User ID
  • Public profile information

1.3 User-Generated Content

When you use our service to create and schedule posts, we temporarily store:

  • Video files and images you upload
  • Caption text and descriptions
  • Scheduled posting times
  • Hashtags and metadata

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Posting and scheduling content to your connected social media accounts as requested by you
  • Managing your OAuth connections to social platforms
  • Providing you with scheduled post management functionality
  • Maintaining service functionality and security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or content with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and user credentials are stored securely using industry-standard encryption methods.

User-generated content (videos, images, captions) may be stored temporarily on our servers solely for the purpose of scheduled posting. Content is processed and deleted according to your settings and platform requirements.

Security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • OAuth tokens are retained until you revoke access or delete your account
  • User-generated content is retained until posting is complete or you request deletion
  • Account information is retained for the duration of your active use of our service

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

5.1 Revoke Access

You can revoke our access to your social media accounts at any time through:

  • The connected platforms' own settings (e.g., TikTok app settings, Instagram connected apps)
  • Disconnecting accounts within our service

5.2 Delete Your Data

To request deletion of your data from our servers:

  • Delete your account through our platform settings
  • Contact us at pcry1111@gmail.com with your request

We will respond to data deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share data with third parties only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Social Media Platforms: To post content as you direct, we share necessary content with TikTok, Instagram, YouTube via their official APIs
  • Service Providers: Our hosting infrastructure providers may process data, bound by appropriate data processing agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our service may use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside of your country of service, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
